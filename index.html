ABOUT ME

Izzy Casey is a poet and editor based in NYC. Her work has been published in or is forthcoming from Gulf Coast: A Journal of Literature and Fine Arts, Black Warrior Review, The Iowa Review, Bennington Review, BOAAT, The Volta, Prelude, NY Tyrant, and elsewhere. She received her MFA in Poetry from the Iowa Writers’ Workshop, where she was the recipient of a fellowship with the Poetry Foundation. Her plays have been produced and developed at Bennington College and for What Happens: A Festival of Poet’s Theatre.
